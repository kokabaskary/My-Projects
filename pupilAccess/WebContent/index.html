Welcome to FJDC
Fatima Jinnah Dental College believes in building skills for success. By offering a high-quality educational experience combined with a welcoming atmosphere, FJDC is positioned to assist you with meeting your educational and professional needs. Our Pre clinical campus located in Korangi Creek, it is just a short 15 minute drive from D.H.A. or P.E.C.H.S. A “State of the Art” purpose built huge campus is the ideal for students to study, play, and learn while working towards their degree. Our admission officers are always available to help you learn more about our faculty, academic programs, and facilities.
