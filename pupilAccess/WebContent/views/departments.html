Clinical

Clinical
The clinical campus comprises of two separate buildings facing each other on 100 Foot Road, Azam Town, adjacent to Phase I, Defence Housing Authority. Here the clinical dental teaching and training is undertaken with great enthusiasm and the students are exposed to a large number of cases on a daily basis. The adjoining localities makeup a very healthy catchment area, where the hospital is within easy reach for over two million people. This strategic location together with the excellent reputation, spanning over 20 years of clinical care, make it a top dental training centre in Pakistan. The other building is the recently developed Azam Town General Hospital, which has been specially geared up for the teaching of general medicine and general surgery to the third professional BDS students. This facility is for the training and teaching of BDS students alone and not to be shared by MBBS students. The exposure to medical and surgical cases is maximized. This unique advantage ensures highest standard of unmatched education, clinical teaching and training.
